JavaZone 2026 Conference Guide

JavaZone is the annual Java conference held in Oslo, Norway.
The conference features talks, workshops, and lightning talks on Java, JVM languages, and related technologies.
Attendees can network with developers from across the Nordic region and beyond.

Venue:
JavaZone takes place at Oslo Spektrum in central Oslo.
The venue is easily accessible by public transport.

Sessions:
The conference runs over two full days with multiple tracks running in parallel.
Topics include Java, Kotlin, security, cloud, architecture, and developer tools.

Food and drinks are provided throughout the conference day.
