JavaZone 2026 Conference Program

JavaZone takes place at Oslo Spektrum, Oslo, Norway on September 9-10, 2026.
Doors open at 08:00. Keynote starts at 09:00 in Hall A.
Sessions run from 09:30 to 18:00 each day with a lunch break from 12:00 to 13:00.

Venue Information:

Oslo Spektrum is located at Sonja Henies plass 2, 0185 Oslo.
The venue is a short walk from Oslo Central Station (Oslo S).
Multiple tram and bus lines stop nearby.

Rooms:
- Hall A: main stage, capacity 1200
- Hall B: secondary stage, capacity 600
- Room 1: workshop and deep-dive room, capacity 150
- Room 2: workshop and deep-dive room, capacity 150

All rooms have power strips at every seat and strong wifi coverage.
